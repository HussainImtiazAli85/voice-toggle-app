🎤 Voice Control

🎤 Auto-starting voice recognition...

📢 Try these voice commands:

  • toggle box 2 - Toggle box 2
  • untoggle box 2 - Turn OFF box 2
  • box 3 - Toggle box 3
  • toggle 5 - Toggle box 5
  • switch box 1 - Toggle box 1
  • turn on box 4 - Turn ON box 4
  • turn off box 6 - Turn OFF box 6
  • two box - Toggle box 2 (spoken numbers)
  • all on - Turn all boxes ON
  • reset or all off - Turn all boxes OFF
  • stop or stop listening - Stop listening
  • 💡 Tip: Say numbers clearly - "two" not "to"
